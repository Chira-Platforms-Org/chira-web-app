Platform

A map-first platform for regional food coordination.

Locality is not designed to be another static marketplace list. It is built as a spatial coordination layer where farms and buyers can see local supply, demand, harvest windows, pricing, and regional opportunities in context.

01

Supply Visibility

Farms can post available products, expected harvest windows, pricing, and minimum order quantities.

02

Demand Discovery

Businesses can publish recurring procurement needs and identify nearby farms that match their sourcing goals.

03

Local Trust

Profiles, certifications, location context, and availability details help buyers understand where their food comes from.
