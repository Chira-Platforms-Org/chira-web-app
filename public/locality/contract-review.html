Flexible Ordering Supply Agreement

This Flexible Ordering Supply Agreement (the “Agreement”) is made and entered into as of May 30, 2026, by and between Queen Creek Harvest (“Seller”) and Roosevelt Row Market (“Buyer”).

  1. Agreement Type. This Agreement establishes flexible ordering terms under which Buyer may place orders for approved products during the agreement term. Buyer is not required to purchase a fixed quantity of each product unless specifically stated in Exhibit A.
  2. Approved Products and Pricing. Seller agrees to make the approved products listed in Exhibit A available to Buyer subject to availability, minimum order requirements, product specifications, and the pricing terms stated in this Agreement.
  3. Ordering Rules. Buyer may select quantities for each order, provided each order satisfies the agreed minimum order requirements. Buyer shall provide at least 48 hours of notice before the requested delivery or pickup window.
  4. Delivery and Fulfillment. Orders accepted under this Agreement shall be fulfilled by Supplier delivery. The standard delivery window shall be Tuesday / Thursday, 8 AM – 11 AM, unless otherwise agreed by both parties.
  5. Payment Terms. Buyer agrees to pay Seller according to Net 15 payment terms. Payment shall be processed through Locality, unless otherwise agreed by the parties.
  6. Inspection and Acceptance. Buyer shall inspect products promptly upon delivery. Buyer must notify Seller of quality, defect, or discrepancy issues within 48 hours of delivery.
  7. Shortages and Substitutions. If Seller cannot fully fulfill an order due to product availability, Seller shall notify Buyer as soon as reasonably possible. Product substitutions require Buyer approval before fulfillment.
  8. Cancellation and Termination. Either party may request cancellation of this Agreement with 14 days written notice, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  9. Custom Terms. The following custom terms shall apply if provided: Buyer may request crop substitutions only if approved in writing by Seller before fulfillment.
  10. Platform Record. This Agreement was generated through Locality Contract Studio as a structured agreement preview. Submission records, message references, version history, and fulfillment activity may be maintained in Locality.
