Flexible Ordering Supply Agreement

This Flexible Ordering Supply Agreement (the “Agreement”) is made and entered into as of May 30, 2026, by and between Queen Creek Harvest (“Seller”) and Roosevelt Row Market (“Buyer”).

  1. Agreement Type. This Agreement establishes flexible ordering terms under which Buyer may place orders for approved products during the agreement term. Buyer is not required to purchase a fixed quantity of each product unless specifically stated in Exhibit A.
  2. Approved Products and Pricing. Seller agrees to make the approved products listed in Exhibit A available to Buyer, subject to availability, minimum order requirements, product specifications, and the pricing terms stated in this Agreement.
  3. Ordering Rules. Buyer may select quantities for each order, provided each order satisfies the agreed minimum order requirements. Buyer shall provide at least 48 hours of notice before the requested delivery or pickup window.
  4. Delivery and Fulfillment. Orders accepted under this Agreement shall be fulfilled by Supplier delivery. The standard delivery or pickup window shall be Tuesday / Thursday, 8 AM – 11 AM, unless otherwise agreed by both parties.
  5. Payment Terms. Buyer agrees to pay Seller according to Net 15 payment terms. Payment shall be processed through Locality, unless otherwise agreed by the parties.
  6. Inspection and Acceptance. Buyer shall inspect products promptly upon delivery. Buyer must notify Seller of any quality, defect, shortage, or discrepancy issue within 48 hours of delivery. Products not disputed within that window may be treated as accepted.
  7. Shortages and Substitutions. If Seller cannot fully fulfill an order due to product availability, weather, harvest conditions, or other operational constraints, Seller shall notify Buyer as soon as reasonably possible. Product substitutions require Buyer approval before fulfillment unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  8. Product Quality, Warranty, and Food Safety. Seller represents that products delivered under this Agreement will be market-grade, reasonably consistent with the specifications in Exhibit A, and handled in accordance with applicable food safety and handling requirements. Except as expressly stated in this Agreement, no additional warranties are made.
  9. Liability Limitation. Each party is responsible for its own acts, omissions, and compliance obligations. To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, Seller’s liability for defective, nonconforming, or undelivered products is limited to replacement of the affected products, credit, refund, or another remedy agreed by the parties.
  10. Cancellation and Termination. Either party may request cancellation or termination of this Agreement with 14 days written notice, unless otherwise agreed in writing. Open orders already accepted before cancellation may remain subject to the fulfillment and payment terms of this Agreement unless both parties agree otherwise.
  11. Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of Arizona, unless another governing jurisdiction is agreed in writing by both parties.
  12. Locality Platform Record and Fees. This Agreement was generated through Locality Contract Studio as a structured agreement preview. Submission records, message references, version history, and fulfillment activity may be maintained in Locality. Seller-paid Locality platform fees are governed by the applicable Locality seller agreement, platform terms, or fee schedule. Locality is not a party to this Buyer-Seller Agreement unless expressly stated in a separate written agreement.
